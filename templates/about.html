About the Project

Final Year Research Initiative

Developing cutting-edge AI solutions for medical and Islamic information retrieval and decision support.

Project Objectives

  • Develop a Swarm-Based Multi-Agent architecture capable of querying medical knowledge bases
  • Implement Multimodal processing of medical and Islamic data
  • Create a dynamic knowledge updating mechanism

Expected Outcomes

  • Improved accuracy and depth in retrieval for both medical and Islamic queries
  • Better handling of complex, multimodal queries
  • Open-source components for academic research
